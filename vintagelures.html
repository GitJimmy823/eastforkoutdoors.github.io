Vintage Rapala Lures Description

We specialize in vintage Fat Rap and Risto Rap models. Our stock consist of brand new lures in the box with
hang tabs for collectors, as well as slightly used lures for the weekend fisherman. We are constantly receiving
new shipments of Vintage Rapala Lures, so please contact us to see if we have the lures you are looking for.
