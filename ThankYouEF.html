Thank You For Your Order!

If you have just placed an order,
We'll respond as soon as possible!

In case you missed it... Our ordering process is divided into 2 steps.

Step 1: "Place Your Order" for lures. You were NOT required to pay for your order at this time.
Upon receiving your order request, we attempt to fill every order to its entirety, but if your order can
not be filled completely, we will NOT back order the remaining items of your order and only charge for
the product we ship to you. Shipping & handling is then calculated for what can be filled in your order.

Step 2: Once your order has been processed, a confirmation email will be sent to you containing
an itemized list of the lures in your order and a total amount that includes shipping and handling.
A notice is included that you will receive an invoice from PayPal that when paid, completes your order.

Merchandise normally arrives in 3-6 business days after payment for your order is received.
