Methods of Payment


EFO uses Paypal to process online purchases of our lures.

Ordering Process, Confirmation & Delivery

Our ordering process is divided into 2 steps.

Step 1: "Place Your Order" for lures. You are NOT required to pay for your order at this time.
Upon receiving your order request, we attempt to fill every order to its entirety, but if your order can
not be filled completely, we will NOT back order the remaining items of your order and only charge for
the product we ship to you. Shipping & handling is then calculated for what can be filled in your order.

Step 2: Once your order has been processed, a confirmation email will be sent to you containing
an itemized list of the lures in your order and a total amount that includes shipping and handling.
A notice is included that you will receive an invoice from PayPal that when paid, completes your order.

Merchandise normally arrives in 3-6 business days after payment for your order is received.
Orders are sent US Postal Service Parcel Post or at your request, by UPS or FedEx.
Expedited delivery is available with priority shipping at additional cost.
International shipping cost will vary by your location and carrier.

Satisfaction Guarantee, Back Orders & Return Policy

Every item we sell has a 100% satisfaction guarantee with a 30 day return policy.
In the unlikely event that you are dissatisfied with your purchase, please contact our Customer Service
Department: eastforkoutdoors@verizon.net. If the item you wanted is out of stock, please contact us for an
expected time of availability of the item. We will email you when the item is again in stock. If you are not
completely satisfied with your order when it arrives, we'll refund your money or exchange any item, but
it must be returned with the original packaging and in the original condition. Please use our contact us form
before returning any item with a full explanation of the problem. If we tell you to return the merchandise,
then fill out the return form which we will supply to you. Using the return shipping label provided, send the
package US Postal Service Parcel Post, UPS ground or FedEx. For your protection, please insure the package
for its full value. COD's will not be accepted. If your package was damaged in shipment by the carrier,
please file a claim with the carrier. Some items sold are covered by a manufacturer's warranty, so...
Please return these items to the items manufacturer for replacement or repair.

If we make a mistake on your order, your return shipping charges will be reimbursed!
